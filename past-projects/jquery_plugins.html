Humble Beginnings

Hakum Wium Lie first proposed an idea called Cascading Style Sheets, aka CSS, in 1994. Shortly after, Lie partnered up with Bert Bos, who created his own stylesheet language when he created the Argo browser.The two began working out a more detailed specification, eventually turning to the newly created HTML working group at the W3C for help. In 1996, CSS had arrived.
